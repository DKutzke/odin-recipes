Blueberry Lemonade

Description

This tasty blueberry lemonade is so refreshing on a hot day! It's easy to make from scratch by blending fresh blueberries with lemon juice, your favorite sweetener, and water.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1/4 cup stevia or your choice of sweetener, to taste
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 9 cups water, or more as needed

Steps:

  1. Combine blueberries, sweetener, lemon juice, and 1 cup water together in a blender; blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a pitcher.
  3. Add remaining water and stir.
  4. Serve chilled or over ice.
