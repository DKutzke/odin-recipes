Lasagna

Description

An easy lasagna recipe that saves me lots of time. This dish satisfies everyone in the family. Try it tonight!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses (cottage cheese, mozzarella and Parmesan)
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings (dried parsley, salt and pepper)
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water 1/2 cup
